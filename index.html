About Me

Upon realising I wanted to pivot to a more technical career, I initially undertook The Odin Project to give myself a foothold in starting a career in web development before developing practical experience in the field.

Having worked in both technical and non-technical Agile teams, I am now equipped with a clear idea of what people working on both sides of the fence need, and how to effectively communicate with them to achieve this, making me a strong candidate for contributing to cross-functional teams, with a keen eye for accessible UX/UI to accompany this.

My strongest skill isn't necessarily a technical one but a people-related one that I picked up from my manager at The Oxbridge Formula (who is incidentally one of the most admirable and inspiring people I've ever met!) – the best way to empower your colleagues to become better at their roles is by guiding them to the solution rather than handing it directly to them. The impact and benefit I've seen from this alone within my teams has been immeasurable.

I'm currently based in Glasgow. I'm open to remote and – depending on the opportunity – hybrid roles.
